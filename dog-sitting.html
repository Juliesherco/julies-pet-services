Dog Sitting Services

At Julie's Pet Services, we offer flexible dog sitting services that fit around your schedule. Whether you need one visit a day or multiple, we'll visit your home to care for your dogs. If requested, we can even take your dog for a walk during our visits.

Our Dog Sitting Services Include:

  • Feeding and water refills
  • Playtime and attention during each visit
  • Walks upon request, tailored to your dog’s needs
  • Administering medication if required
  • Regular updates with photos
  • 24/7 emergency contact service

Our visits can be scheduled as often as needed, ensuring your dog stays happy and well cared for while you're away.

Pricing

Our dog sitting services start at £30 per visit. Additional visits and dog walks can be added for a customized package. Contact us for a detailed quote based on your needs.
