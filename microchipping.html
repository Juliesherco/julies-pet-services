Microchipping Services

At Julie’s Pet Scanning and Microchipping, I offer a professional microchipping service throughout Neath, Port Talbot, Swansea, and Aberdare. Microchipping is a crucial step in ensuring your pet's safety and compliance with UK law. It is a legal requirement for dogs to be microchipped by the breeder from six weeks old, before they leave for their new homes. I provide a convenient and stress-free service to meet this obligation at a day and time that suits you.

Our microchipping process is quick, safe, and effective. Each microchip is registered and accurately linked to your contact details. This ensures that if your pet ever goes missing, they can be quickly and easily identified and reunited with you. I am dedicated to providing the best care and support for your furry friends.
